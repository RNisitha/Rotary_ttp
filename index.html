About Our Club

Rotary Club of Thiruthuraipoondi Green City is a dynamic community service organization dedicated to making a meaningful difference through compassion, leadership, and collaboration. As part of the global Rotary family, our club works tirelessly to address local needs while contributing to international humanitarian efforts.

Our members come from diverse professional backgrounds, united by a shared commitment to serving society. Together, we organize impactful projects, build meaningful partnerships, and create opportunities that improve lives and strengthen our community.

Areas of Service

Education

We support students through educational assistance, school improvement initiatives, learning resources, scholarships, and mentorship programs.

Healthcare

Our club organizes medical camps, blood donation drives, health awareness campaigns, and wellness initiatives that promote healthier communities.

Environmental Sustainability

We are committed to protecting our environment through tree plantation drives, clean-up campaigns, water conservation, and sustainable community projects.

Community Development

We work closely with local communities by supporting welfare programs, disaster relief efforts, and initiatives that improve the quality of life for families.

Youth Development

We encourage young people to become future leaders by providing leadership programs, skill development opportunities, career guidance, and community engagement activities.

Vocational Service

We help individuals develop professional skills, provide career guidance, organize vocational training programs, and create employment opportunities.
